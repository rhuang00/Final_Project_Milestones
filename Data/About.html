Background

The NCAA Division I Women's Volleyball Tournament, since 1981, is an annual competition that leads to the championship in women's volleyball from college teams in Division I every winter. Stanford won the most recent tournament in 2019, defeating Wisconsin 3-0 in Pittsburgh.
